ADMISSION PROCEDURE

The candidate can apply for course by filling the admission form on the website along with that they need to upload the scanned copy of required document.

ELIGIBILITY AND COURSES

Minimum qualification for securing admission to Certificate Level Courses is 10th and Minimum Qualification for securing Admission in Diploma is 10+2 passed in Science stream for Paramedical and 10+2 passed in any stream for Hotel Management.
